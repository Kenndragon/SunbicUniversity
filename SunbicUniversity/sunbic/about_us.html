About Us
HISTORY
SUNBIC University, is one of the top University located in Indonesia. until this year, we have over 10.000 undergraduate and postgraduate students. SUNBIC University started on March 3rd 2002. Back in 2002, we were only located in North Jakarta and we only had 4 faculties which is:
  • Faculty of Engineering
  • Faculty of Economics and Business
  • Faculty of Computer Science
  • Faculty of Medicine
In late 2019, since we began to have more students, SUNBIC University decided to built a second campus in Surabaya. We also have added 3 more faculties to SUNBIC University which is:
  • Faculty of Art and Design
  • Faculty of Law
  • Faculty of Psychology
Now in 2022, we have 5 campuses in total. One in North Jakarta, one in Central Jakarta, one in Surabaya and the rest is in Malang and Bali. SUNBIC University now, has around 2.000 staff to help more than 10.000 students from over 20 countries around the world.
VISION & MISSION
Vision
Our vision is to become a top university in Indonesia that is able to maximize the potential of students and help them achieve their dreams.
Mission
Our mission is to educate the students to develop not only their cognitive, but also their character so that they can contribute to their country with integrity and excellence.
